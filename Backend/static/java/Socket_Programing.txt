TCP/IP (Transmission Control Protocol / Internet Protocol)
______________________________________________________________

TCP/IP un protocols ka ek group hai jo internet ya kisi bhi network pe data ko transfer karne ke rules define karta hai

yani TCP/IP ek protocol suite hai jiska use internet aur computer networks me communication ke liye hota hai
Iska kaam data ko reliably ek computer se doosre computer tak bhejna hai

TCP/IP is a suite of communication protocols used to interconnect network devices on the internet
It breaks data into packets (TCP) and ensures it reaches the correct address (IP) reliably and in order


TCP/IP ki Zarurat
_________________________

Socho 1960s-70s ka zamana Computers alag-alag jagah lage hue the kisi university me, kisi army base me, kisi space center me
Ye computers ek doosre se baat nahi kar paate the
Har machine ka apna alag system, apna protocol hota tha
Agar IBM ka computer aur Xerox ka computer hote, to wo aapas me baat nahi kar paate

Problem ye thi ki Har company apna protocol bana rahi thi koi proprietary language jise dusra samajh hi nahi pata tha. Is wajah se inter-communication impossible ho raha tha

1973 USA Department of Defense ka ARPANET project
______________________________________________________________
Ek aisa common system develop karna jo har computer chahe koi bhi ho ek doosre se baat kar sake

unho ne TCP/IP protocol suite bana liya jo 1983 me officially standard bana

TCP vs IP -> TCP Data ko chhote packets me todta hai IP Packet ko sahi address tak bhejne ka kaam karta hai


example -> Tum ek gift box bhejna chahte ho USA me kisi dost ko Tumhara TCP box ko parts me divide karta hai aur numbering karta hai Tumhara IP har part pe correct address likhta hai Courier company har part ko deliver karti hai Wahan tumhara dost sare parts collect karke TCP se unko original shape me wapas jodta hai

Ye 2 major protocols par based hai:

1 -> TCP (Transmission Control Protocol) – Reliable communication

2 -> IP (Internet Protocol) – Addressing aur routing


TCP/IP ke 4 layers hote hain

1 -> Application Layer
2 -> Transport Layer
3 -> Internet Layer
4 -> Network Access Layer
